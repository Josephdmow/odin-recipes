Grilled Pizza

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. pizza dough
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for brushing and drizzling
  • 1/2 lb. thinly sliced mozzarella
  • 1 large tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1 c. cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1c. ricotta
  • Freshly chopped basil, for serving
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

Directions

  1. Heat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the pizza dough into 2 equal-sized balls.
  3. Use your hands to stretch each dough ball into a circle that's approximately 8 inches wide.
  4. Brush both sides of the dough circles with oil to prevent sticking.
  5. Place the oiled dough circles directly on the grill grates.
  6. Cook the dough until the bottom is golden, which usually takes 1 to 2 minutes.
  7. Carefully flip the dough to cook the other side.
  8. Immediately top the crust with half of the cheese, tomatoes, and ricotta.
  9. Continue cooking until the bottom of the pizza is golden, which typically takes about another minute.
  10. Repeat the process with the remaining dough ball to make the second pizza.
  11. Top with basil, red pepper flakes, and a drizzle of oil.
